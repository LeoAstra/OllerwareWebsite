The basic idea is to drag an arrow out of the player. The run the symulation.

Currently this is just a placeholder, but within the next week I will start linking tutorials for the various levels and aspects of the game. The media team at Ollerware has been working very diligently to pull this off.


Since I am the programmer, all I can do is answer the question at hand. Click on the player and drag the arrow in the direction you wish, for the magnitude you wish. If you let go of the arrow, it will stop drawing. Once you have the desired arrow you simply press the Symulate button and watch it go.
