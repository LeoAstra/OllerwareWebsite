Who is behind Ollerware?

My name is Louis Oller and I am the founder of Ollerware.


I am an 18 year old Indie Game developer. Having started out programming functions for Haverhill Robotics I quickly branched out into game development with python3. As a junior I took AP Computer Science which set me up with the skillset to intern at an educational gaming company known as Muzzy Lane. That summer I interned at Omtool, a software company that designed document handling software.


As a senior I wanted to find a way to present to potential employers what projects I worked on, while displaying my senior programming capstone. That is how Ollerware was born.
